About Auto Specialists

Welcome to Auto Specialists, your trusted partner for exceptional auto body repair and painting services in West Haven. With years of experience, we are dedicated to restoring your vehicle to its original glory, ensuring both safety and aesthetics.

Our journey began with a passion for automobiles and a commitment to craftsmanship. As a proud family-owned business, Chris, our owner, established this shop with the vision of providing honest, high-quality repair services that the community could rely on. We understand that being involved in an accident can be stressful, and our goal is to make the repair process as smooth and hassle-free as possible for you.

In our state-of-the-art workshop, we possess the expertise to diagnose and repair all mechanical concerns, backed by our certified technicians. We are your local complete auto body shop and service center, striving for excellence in every repair. At Auto Specialists, we pride ourselves on our attention to detail, use of advanced repair techniques, and friendly customer service. Our team is continuously trained on the latest automotive technologies and repair methods to handle all makes and models, using only the highest quality parts and materials to ensure durability and a perfect finish.

We believe in transparency and open communication. From the initial estimate to the final delivery, we keep you informed every step of the way. Your satisfaction is our top priority, and we stand behind our work with a comprehensive warranty.

Thank you for considering Auto Specialists for your vehicle repair needs. We invite you to stop by and discover the comprehensive services we offer. We look forward to serving you and getting you back on the road safely and quickly.

Trusted Since 1981

For over four decades, Auto Specialists has been the trusted name in vehicle care since 1981. Our seasoned professionals are adept at servicing all makes and models, from compact cars to robust SUVs. We're committed to ensuring your vehicle performs flawlessly, delivering meticulous service every time. Experience the difference of having it done right, the first time.
