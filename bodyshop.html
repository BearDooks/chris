Comprehensive Bodyshop Services

At Auto Specialists, our state-of-the-art bodyshop is equipped to handle all your vehicle's needs, from minor cosmetic repairs to major collision restoration. Our certified technicians combine advanced techniques with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring your vehicle returns to its pre-accident condition, or better.

We pride ourselves on using the latest technology, including our advanced downdraft paint booth, to deliver flawless finishes and perfect color matching every time. Trust us to restore your vehicle's integrity and appearance with unparalleled craftsmanship.

Our Bodyshop Expertise

Collision Repair

Our expert team specializes in comprehensive collision repair, addressing structural damage, panel replacement, and chassis alignment. We work diligently to restore your vehicle's safety and performance after any impact.

  • Frame Straightening & Alignment
  • Panel Replacement & Repair
  • Bumper Repair & Replacement
  • Unibody Repair

Auto Painting & Refinishing

Achieve a factory-quality finish with our advanced auto painting services. Our downdraft paint booth ensures a dust-free environment for perfect color matching and durable, high-gloss results.

  • Precision Color Matching
  • Scratch, Scuff & Chip Repair
  • Full Vehicle Repaints
  • Clear Coat Application

Dent & Ding Removal

From minor door dings to significant hail damage, we offer various dent removal techniques, including paintless dent repair (PDR) to preserve your original paintwork whenever possible.

  • Paintless Dent Repair (PDR)
  • Hail Damage Repair
  • Crease & Minor Dent Removal

Headlight Restoration

Improve visibility and the aesthetic appeal of your vehicle with our professional headlight restoration services. We remove oxidation and haziness, restoring clarity and brightness.
